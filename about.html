Full Introduction

Megan is a selfless 23 year old woman from North Platte Nebraska who has aspirations of inventing countless products that benefit millions of people. Megan has a small family that consists of her husband, herself, and their dog. Currently she works as a part time secretary while she attends Mid Plains Community College full time. In the summer semester of 2024, she plans to transfer her studies to Arizona State University, where she will study mechanical engineering full time. When she completes her studies, she hopes to land an engineering job at a product development/product testing company so she can learn the engineering best practices that will help her get closer to reaching her goals. In her free time Megan enjoys painting her nails, as well as others nails, as a form of artistic expression. She also enjoys taking part in active activities to stay fit, such as running, in an effort to remain in good health. One of Megan's goals for 2023 was to travel more because it is something that she enjoys doing often. She met this goal and ended up traveling to California five times, Hawaii once, Missouri once, Texas once, and Virginia once all in 2023. In 2024 Megan has goals of keeping high grades in the upper level math classes that she is assigned to do as a part of her curriculum, lose 10 pounds, traveling more with aspirations of possibly going out of the country to Europe, and lastly she is preparing to hopefully finish a marathon that takes place in march.
